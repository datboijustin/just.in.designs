Project Description

ParkPulse: Your mobile parking companion with live data and hot spots personalized to your commute.

PROJECT BRIEF: ParkPulse's intention is to help create a better experience during an user's commute. Through assumptions, research, and a competitive analysis during the Define and Ideation phase, the user needs were concluded to be a more cost-effective commute, a way to eliminate excess loss of time, and a more time-efficient way to park to reach their destination on time.

THE PROBLEM: Driving to large downtown capitals and the return of office jobs since the pandemic causes issues among city infrastructure including congested traffic and parking problems. While 54 hours of lost time per year is attributed to traffic congestion, 80% of users also feel that parking is a hassle when commuting downtown. Additionally, the nature of urban infrastructure with one-way streets, increase of bike lanes, and insufficient signage makes downtown commuting and driving difficult.

THE PROPOSAL: After conducting user interviews, preparing an affinity diagram, and outlining an Eisenhower Matrix to understand a user's empathy while commuting, ParkPulse offers 4 main features to improve the state of a user's desire to commute. ParkPulse offers on-the-go parking data that will adjust the user’s route based on their needs to save time and money. The second main feature will allow users to create custom commute presets that will adapt to their habits. The third main feature will contain a streamlined reservation system with integrated tap-to-pay, which offers an efficient reserve parking experience. The 4th main feature offers an in-app credit system that offers promotional discounts the more a user parks with ParkPulse to create a better overall app experience for the user.
